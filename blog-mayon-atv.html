Your Ultimate Guide to the Mayon ATV Adventure

There are many ways to experience the majestic Mayon Volcano, but nothing quite compares to riding an ATV through its dramatic lava fields and rugged trails. Whether you are an adventure junkie or just looking for a unique way to see this natural wonder up close, the Mayon ATV Adventure is an experience you will not forget.

What to Expect

The ATV trails wind through solidified lava fields, river crossings, and open terrain with Mayon Volcano looming ahead of you the entire time. Most tours take between 1 to 2 hours and are led by a local guide who knows the terrain well. The views are nothing short of spectacular.

What to Wear and Bring

  • Comfortable clothes you don't mind getting dusty
  • Closed-toe shoes or sneakers — sandals are not allowed
  • Sunscreen and a hat or buff for dust and sun
  • A camera or GoPro (mounts are usually available for rent)
  • Bottled water — you will need it

Getting There from Bali Suites

The main ATV jump-off point in Camalig is approximately 20–25 minutes from Bali Suites Legazpi. We recommend going early in the morning — the light is golden, the air is cooler, and Mayon is usually clearest before noon. Our front desk can help arrange a tricycle or jeepney transfer for you.

Tips for First-Timers

  • Book ahead, especially on weekends and holidays — slots fill up fast
  • Listen carefully to the safety briefing before you ride
  • Go at your own pace — there is no rush, and the photo spots are worth the stops
  • Combine it with a visit to the Cagsawa Ruins on the same day for a full adventure itinerary
