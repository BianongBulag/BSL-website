Roof Deck Pool

A refreshing rooftop escape above the city.

Cool Off Above the City

Our Roof Deck Pool is one of Legazpi City's most refreshing getaways. Perched on the upper level of Bali Suites, the pool offers a cool dip paired with open-air views of the surrounding city and the magnificent Mayon Volcano in the distance.

Perfect for families, kids, and anyone who wants to unwind without leaving the property. Whether you're a morning swimmer or an afternoon lounger, the roof deck is your go-to spot.
