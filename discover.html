Discover Legazpi

Explore the City of Fun and Adventure.

Mayon ATV Adventure

Get up close and personal with the world's most perfect volcanic cone. Ride an ATV through thrilling lava trails, rivers, and volcanic rocks, just 20 minutes from our suites.

Cagsawa Ruins

Step back in time at this iconic historical landmark. The remnants of a 16th-century Franciscan church provide one of the best and most dramatic framing shots of Mount Mayon.

Spicy Bicolano Food

Indulge in the rich, spicy flavors of Bicol. From authentic Bicol Express to the famous Sili Ice Cream, Legazpi City is a food lover's paradise, with top restaurants located just a short drive away.
